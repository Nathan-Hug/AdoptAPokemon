Our Mission

When you adopt a Pokemon, you gain a life long friend. Here at Adopt a Pokemon, our goal is for the community to adopt'em all. All of the Pokemon here at our shelter were rescued.

History & Info

Our shelter has been serving the community for over 100 years. Our staff are trained to not only help you find your perfect life long companion, but also provide you with the right tools and information you need to feel comfortable with your adoption.
